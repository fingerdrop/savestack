The SaveStack Solution:

Your Vendor Operations Partner for Every Building in the Portfolio

SaveStack becomes your behind-the-scenes force for operational clarity and cost recovery—while your team stays focused on high-value property performance.

Here’s how we help:

01

Unified Billing & Clear Reporting

We consolidate your vendor invoices into one clear monthly bill—with spend broken out by program, department, or funding source. Easier for your bookkeeper. Cleaner for your board.

02

Insider Rates on Everyday Services

We’ve pre-negotiated pricing with hundreds of vendors—telecom, janitorial, waste, IT, and more—so you pay less without changing providers. Real savings that free up budget for programs.

03

Contract Oversight & Negotiation

No more auto-renewals or forgotten contracts. We track your renewals, benchmark your rates, and step in to renegotiate when needed—all while you stay focused on your mission.

04

Operational Support That Saves Staff Time

Need to update a vendor’s hours, cancel a service, or resolve a billing issue? Just tell us once. We’ll handle it, document it, and follow up—so your staff doesn’t have to.

Cut Vendor Spend With Insider Rates

Contract Negotiation & Lifecycle Management

No more surprise auto-renewals or outdated terms. We monitor contract expirations, benchmark pricing, and renegotiate on your behalf—with your approval.

Unified Billing That Actually Makes Sense

Vendor Helpdesk for Your Team
