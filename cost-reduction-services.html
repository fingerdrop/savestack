Frequently asked question

Everything You Need to Know

What types of vendors does SaveStack manage?

We specialize in SaaS and software vendors. Think Slack, HubSpot, Zoom, design tools, analytics platforms, and security software. We don't handle utilities or shipping.

How is this different from hiring a procurement consultant?

Procurement consultants audit and leave. We stick around. You get ongoing vendor support, contract tracking, and daily operations management, not just a one-time analysis.

Do I lose control of my vendor accounts?

No. You keep full admin access and final approval on all decisions. We handle the coordination and negotiations, but you stay in control of your tools and data.

How do you ensure savings stick long-term?

We track every renewal date and renegotiate rates before they expire. Plus, we monitor usage patterns to catch license creep and redundant tools before they bloat your budget.
